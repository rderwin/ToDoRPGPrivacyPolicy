‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn XP?
Complete tasks by tapping on them! Must Do tasks give 10 XP, Should Do tasks give 20 XP, and Could Do tasks give 40 XP. You can also adjust XP values using the +/- buttons on each task.
What are perfect day streaks?
A perfect day occurs when you complete all relevant tasks for that day. Streaks give you XP multipliers - the longer your streak, the more bonus XP you earn (up to 5x at 30+ days)!
How do I customize what counts for perfect days?
Go to the Stats screen and scroll down to "Perfect Day Settings". You can choose whether Should Do and Could Do items count toward your perfect day streaks.
Can I view tasks from previous days?
Yes! Use the calendar view to see your productivity history. Tap on any day to view tasks from that date, or use the date navigation arrows to browse through days.
How do I delete a task?
Tap the trash icon on the right side of any task to delete it. The trash icon appears when you tap on a task.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
Your data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all your progress, tasks, and statistics will be lost. We recommend backing up your device regularly.

üîß Troubleshooting

App crashes or won't open

  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have the latest version of iOS
  4. Check if you have enough storage space available

Tasks not saving or disappearing

  1. Make sure you're connected to the internet
  2. Try adding a new task to see if the issue persists
  3. Restart the app
  4. Check if you have sufficient storage space

XP calculations seem wrong

  1. Check your Perfect Day Settings in the Stats screen
  2. Make sure you're completing tasks on the correct day
  3. Verify that your streak is being calculated correctly
  4. Try refreshing the app by going to another day and back

Calendar not showing correct data

  1. Pull down to refresh the calendar view
  2. Navigate to a different day and back
  3. Check if the date you're viewing has any tasks
  4. Restart the app if the issue persists
